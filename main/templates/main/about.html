ABOUT US

Thesharptenant is a social innovation powered by systemshift.io is Nigeria's Number 1 search engine for properties, aimed at helping prospective tenants get insights into properties before they pay for them even in thier busy schedule. We give our users the ability to find the state of properties so as to avoid paying for houses with unpaid PHCN bills by previous occupants, apileptic water and power supply, flood prone houses, and abusive landlords. We are not a real estate agent, but we're committed to balancing power in the Real Estate market and ensure, Landlords do the right things and tenants are not hoodwinked.

We have engineered a next-to-none more convenient and effective way for prospective tenants and property seekers to find an up-to-date property information, and also to allow people review houses they have lived in, to give incoming tenants an idea of what they are coming for. The goal is to create an open appraisal system to ensure that Tenants are well informed, thereby putting to check irresponsible Landlords and Agents.
